About Cossieplay

  • CossiePlay is a family-run business, operating in Hulkstown since 2012.
  • We make costumes and accessories (second-hand also) which can be ordered under "Gallery".
  • Collection period (1 - 50 weeks) and costs ($10 - $10,000) are based on design and materials

Events

  • On the first Saturday of every month we hold a “CossiePlay Party” at the town hall from 6pm - 11pm
  • $15 for adults, $10 for ages 6-17 and kids under 6 are free.
  • Each entry includes one non-alcoholic drink and a glow stick.

Come and dance with a live DJ on set! Songs can be requested.

Information about cosplay

  • Cosplay (costume play) is a performance art that refers to participants (cosplayers) dressing up as a specific character.

  • Cosplayers (often genders swtiched) often interact to create a subculture.

  • Favorite sources include manga and anime, comic books and cartoons, video games, and live-action films and television series.

  • There is an increase in cosplayers since 1990. Undoubtedly popular in Japan, some other parts of Asia and Western countries.

  • Cosplay events are usually organised by fans that hold local and international competitions through social networking platforms
